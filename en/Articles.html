List of films

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone


The film follows Harry Potter, a young orphan discovering that he is a wizard, and his first year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets


The second year of Harry at Hogwarts, where he investigates strange events related to the legendary Chamber of Secrets.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban


Harry discovers truths about his past while seeking to protect the school from a dangerous escaped criminal, Sirius Black.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire


Harry is involuntarily entered into a dangerous Triwizard Tournament and discovers new mysteries about the return of Lord Voldemort.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince


The origins of the Dark Lord are revealed, and Harry and his friends prepare for the final battle against him.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 1


Harry, Ron, and Hermione flee and search for the Horcruxes, objects that contain parts of Voldemort's soul.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 2


The epic conclusion of the saga, where Harry confronts Voldemort and the destinies of the characters are sealed.
